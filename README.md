The Indian Compass - Your Cultural and Spiritual Guide of India
Developed by Team Retros

📖 **Overview**
The Indian Compass is an interactive web platform designed to preserve and promote India’s cultural heritage. It provides a unified digital space to explore traditions, festivals, cuisines, monuments, art forms, and spiritual practices from across the country.
The project aims to reconnect younger generations with Indian culture while offering global audiences an authentic and engaging experience of India’s rich heritage.

💡 **Problem Statement**
India’s vast cultural legacy is often fragmented and underrepresented online. With globalization, younger generations are losing touch with traditional roots, and tourists rarely get to experience India beyond the mainstream attractions.
The Indian Compass bridges this gap by creating a modern, interactive, and AI-powered platform that preserves, educates, and promotes Indian culture in a way that is engaging, authentic, and easily accessible.

🎯 **Objectives**
Cultural Preservation: Digitally record and share diverse traditions, festivals, and art forms.
Engaging Learning: Make learning about Indian culture interactive through stories, images, and maps.
Youth Connection: Present cultural content in a modern, student-friendly format.
Support for Artisans: Connect local craftsmen and vendors with online audiences.
Tourism Promotion: Encourage exploration of heritage sites and festivals with AI-driven recommendations.

🚀 **Features**
🌏 Explore
Discover monuments, temples, festivals, and cuisines through curated visuals and stories.
Access interactive maps to explore heritage sites by state or region.
🤖 AI Assistant
Chat with an AI guide to learn about culture, traditions, and tourist attractions.
Receive personalized cultural recommendations.
📷 AI Scanner
Scan monuments, temples, or handicrafts to get instant insights and historical details.
🛍️ Marketplace
Explore and buy authentic handcrafted products directly from local artisans.
🕉️ Cultural Learning
Interactive educational content designed for students and cultural enthusiasts.
